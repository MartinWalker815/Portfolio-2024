About Me

Hey there!

My name is Martin and I am currently studying to become a front-end developer, specialising in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create interactive and responsive websites.

I’m excited to take on new challenges in web development and apply my diverse skills to create engaging, high-quality websites.
